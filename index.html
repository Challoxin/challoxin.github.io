What the app does

App Functionality

Challoxin allows members of our community to sync their social profiles within our community ecosystem. After signing in, users can link their TikTok account through TikTok's official Login Kit, allowing the app to display their public profile (display name and avatar) and their public TikTok video feed on our community hub, and to connect their identity across our Twitch and Discord community spaces.

When you post a new public video on TikTok, the app can trigger community alerts — such as notifications in our Discord server — so followers never miss new content. The app only reads public data that you have already posted; it never publishes, edits, or deletes content on your behalf. Every connection can be revoked by the user at any time from their account settings or directly within TikTok.
